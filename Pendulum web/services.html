DIRECTOR

Prince Henry Iseghohi

Henry Iseghohi is a highly experienced Executive with over thirty years of Executive time in a variety of businesses.
Henry is currently the MD/CEO of Pendulum Technical Limited. Prior to Project Pendulum, Henry was involved in creating along with others several successful startup’s including M & B Products, Cargo Management Services and Pendulum Technologies Limited.
Henry worked for Brandon, Smith & Jones CPA’S, an Accounting firm located in Memphis Tennessee USA in the 1980’s, providing Audit Services for The Federal Government of the United States. Such Audits included The Environmental protection Agency (EPA), University Financial Aid Audits for College work study, BEOG and SEOG programs, City Audits. Other projects included Small Business Administration (SBA) audits and Accounting services to businesses and institutions.
Henry has a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Accounting/Finance and a Master’s in International Law and Diplomacy. He served the nation in the NYSC program in 1983 with The Federal Ministry of Finance. Henry has continued to attend various Business Seminars all around the globe.

DIRECTOR

Chris Erewele Snr.

Chris is a seasoned and highly rated oil and gas player with extensive reach in the industry particularly in the Balkans, Ukraine and most of the rest of the old Soviet Union. He has spent the last decade operating between London and Lagos with his primary focus on issues that impact the new technological revolution spearheaded by the telecom industry.
Chris is the master of team work and structuring, which has been of remarkable value to him over the years in his ability to make a difference in the sales, marketing and distribution of a variety of product lines he has been involved with. Chris is the Chairman and CEO of E & CEE Industries Ltd, a Nigerian based company with interest in Real Estate. He is also on the board of TOSI Oilfield Services Ltd, an oil services company based in Port Harcourt.
He holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Economic Development, from University of North London and Diploma in Politics and International Relations from the University of Kent in Canterbury. He also holds a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Mass Communications and Journalism, from Clapham College of Technology in London. He holds a Master of Law and International Diplomacy (MILD).

DIRECTOR TECHNICAL SERVICES

Gerber Lazo

Gerber Lazo Ramos brings to Pendulum over 10 years’ experience in the Under-ground drilling Industry. Trained in the El-Salvador Central America, in Drilling Operations and Under Ground equipment Operations, he worked in the USA, El-Salvador, Brazil, China & Nigeria in various Drilling positions for US based Telecom Operator- BROD BAND Communication, BUENCO-SA-de-CV of EL-Salvador and JIANGSU DILONG Heavy Machinery Co. of CHINA. Mr.Lazo is currently The Chief Technical Officer of Pendulum Technical Limited-Nigeria in charge of the Company’s HORIZONTAL DIRECTIONAL DRILLING (HDD) Machines.
Mr.Lazo is a versatile HDD Operator/Locator and has a vast experience and dexterity in handling HDD Machines like VERMEER, Ditch-Witch(DW),& DL of China. He is the first HDD Operator/Locator in Nigeria through Pendulum Technical Limited.
Gerber has a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration at Modular Open University,El-Salvador and Advance International Technologies in Underground Drilling Course in Georgia, USA.
